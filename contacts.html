Hospitality is among the fastest-growing sectors in various countries. It is more evident in developing countries with unexhausted natural resources. My business will be on starting a short-let holiday accommodation facility in my hometown in Ibadan, Nigeria. I intend to build executive houses to take advantage of the ever-increasing demand in this market. The business will be called Home Away From Home, where the primary activity is to provide an atmosphere and comfort of living at home. It will give the best solution to both local and international tourists traveling to the targeted area and the surrounding. Effective marketing strategies will be adopted to create awareness of the products and services offered by the firm. Home Away From Home will emphasize product differentiation in the creation of competitive advantage. Reliable vendors will be outsourced to ensure the provision of high-quality products and services. Home Away From Home will be developed to be a global brand where tourists can book in advance while still in their home country.
